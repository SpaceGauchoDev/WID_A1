Reseña

Star Trek: The Next Generation, often abbreviated to TNG, is the third Star Trek television series, and the first set in the 24th century. Like its predecessors, it was created by Gene Roddenberry. Produced at Paramount Pictures, it aired in first-run syndication, by Viacom in the US, from September 1987 to May 1994. The series led to three spin-offs in the TNG era: Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, which it ran alongside during its final two seasons, Star Trek: Voyager, and Star Trek: Picard. It is also the beginning of a contiguous period of time during which there was always at least one Star Trek series in production, ending with Star Trek: Enterprise in 2005.
